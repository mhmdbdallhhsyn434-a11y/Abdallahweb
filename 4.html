Information Technology:

Information Technology means using computers and software to manage information. It includes hardware, software, and data networks. IT helps people and companies store and share data easily. It improves communication and productivity. IT is important in business, education, and daily life.

Network:

A network connects computers and devices together. It allows users to share files, data, and the Internet. Networks can be wired or wireless. They make communication fast and easy. The Internet is the largest example of a network.

Web Design:

Web design is the process of creating websites. It includes layout, colors, text, and images. Designers use HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. A good design makes the website clear and attractive. Its goal is to give users a good online experience.
